About

Nice Kangaroo Care Kits began as a creative extension of Neha Kamichetty's Nice Kangaroo mascot and a desire to design thoughtful gifts for college students and young professionals. The brand combines warm colors, playful language, and practical products that make everyday life feel a little easier.

Our mission is to help customers celebrate milestones, support friends during stressful weeks, and send meaningful care packages without spending hours searching for individual items. Each kit reflects the friendly, organized spirit of the Nice Kangaroo brand.
