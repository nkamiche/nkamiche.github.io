Website Evaluations

getmyboat

  • Contrast: Very bright colors and pleasing to look at.
  • Repetition: The same design elements are repeated throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Very aligned images and captions, super organized
  • Proximity: Everything spaced out as well as grouped together.

South Town Water Sports

  • Contrast: The site has many different colors, which can make it hard to focus on important elements.
  • Repetition: The site uses a consistent layout and design elements throughout.
  • Alignment: The page feels crowded with all the big words and images.
  • Proximity: There is too much information grouped together, making it hard to navigate.
